Research Interests

Computational Catalysis

Mechanistic study of homogeneous & heterogeneous catalysts. Reaction pathways, transition states, and free energy landscapes.

Bonding & Reactivity

Activation strain model, EDA, charge transfer analysis, and conceptual DFT to unravel chemical bonding.

Photo-physical Properties

Excited state dynamics, absorption/emission spectra, charge-transfer states, and photostability.

Special Topics

Heavy atom tunneling, microkinetic modeling, QM/MM enzyme catalysis, and machine learning potentials.

Tools & methods: ORCA | Gaussian | CP2K | Python | NBO | Multiwfn | VMD
